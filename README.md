# JobApplication
This system is divided into two roles: Jobseeker & Employer

Jobseeker
-Search the job application with the keywords
-View the job list (post by employer)
-Profile (Edit and View)
-Apply the job application with their resume
-View the details of the job application
-View their job application record (Include process)

Employer
-Post the job application
-Job application (View details, Edit, Delete)
-View the details of the jobseeker who apply the job
-Update the apply process of the job application. (Reject, Pending and Accept)
-Download the CV of the jobseekers
-Profile (View and Edit)

Database:
SQLServer
